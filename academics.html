Academics

Educorp University’s Department of Computational Intelligence and Data Technologies offers a comprehensive suite of degree programs across three levels of study — Undergraduate, Master’s, and Doctoral (PhD) — designed to equip learners with deep technical knowledge, research capabilities, and real-world problem-solving skills in data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

Not sure which specialty to pursue? Begin your journey through our Computing Exploratory Studies Pathway (CESP) — a flexible first-year experience that allows you to explore core concepts across all three fields while receiving expert guidance before selecting your major.


Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Data Science


Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Data Science at Educorp University is a forward-looking undergraduate program designed to prepare students for the data-driven future of business, science, and technology. This program combines mathematics, statistics, and computer science with real-world data applications to equip students with the analytical and technical skills required to collect, manage, analyze, and visualize large datasets. Students begin with foundational courses in calculus, linear algebra, and programming, then progress into specialized areas such as statistical modeling, machine learning, big data systems, and data visualization.


Graduates of the program are well-positioned for careers as data scientists, business analysts, research analysts, data engineers, or policy advisors. The degree also serves as a strong foundation for further study in applied statistics, machine learning, or artificial intelligence at the graduate level. With its strong industry linkages, modern computing labs, and a curriculum grounded in ethical and responsible data use, the BSc in Data Science at Educorp University prepares students to lead in the digital economy.

Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence


Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence at Educorp University offers students a deep dive into the world of intelligent systems, autonomous agents, and human-machine interaction. The program combines foundational computer science with specialized AI topics such as natural language processing, robotics, neural networks, computer vision, and knowledge representation. Students are trained to build systems that can learn, reason, and adapt in real time — from self-driving vehicles to recommendation engines.


With a curriculum aligned to global AI standards, learners work on interdisciplinary projects that blend ethical theory, AI regulation, and real-world innovation. They gain hands-on experience using tools such as TensorFlow, OpenCV, and PyTorch while engaging in research and development projects in sectors like healthcare, logistics, and education. By the end of the program, students are equipped to design and deploy AI systems that are ethical, scalable, and impactful.

Bachelor of Science in Machine Learning


Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Machine Learning is a specialized degree focused on algorithms that allow machines to learn from data and improve their performance over time. Students develop expertise in supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and predictive analytics. The curriculum blends core programming, mathematical modeling, and applied statistics with cutting-edge ML techniques used in real-world problem solving.


Students participate in lab-based learning and simulation environments where they train, test, and evaluate models across diverse fields such as speech recognition, fraud detection, climate forecasting, and bioinformatics. With a strong emphasis on both theoretical depth and hands-on experience, the program prepares graduates to become machine learning engineers, quantitative analysts, or AI researchers ready to shape the future of intelligent automation.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with Data Analytics


Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with Data Analytics blends traditional computer science education with modern analytical techniques. Students gain a solid foundation in algorithms, systems design, databases, and programming, while developing advanced skills in data exploration, statistical analysis, and visualization. The program is ideal for students interested in building software and analyzing data to drive decisions.


Practical learning is a core feature, with students using tools like Python, Tableau, and Power BI to solve business and societal challenges. Graduates leave with the ability to not only build computational systems but also derive insights from data—making them well-suited for roles such as software developers, data analysts, systems engineers, or tech consultants in data-driven industries.

Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics and AI


Program Overview

The BSc in Business Analytics and AI equips students with a dual skillset in data-driven business strategy and intelligent systems. The program bridges business acumen and technical proficiency, offering coursework in market analytics, machine learning, financial modeling, and decision support systems. Students explore how AI technologies can transform industries and improve organizational performance.


With real-world case studies and industry collaborations, students learn to apply AI techniques to marketing, operations, supply chains, and customer engagement. Graduates are prepared for roles such as business intelligence analysts, AI strategy consultants, or product data managers in high-growth sectors like fintech, retail, and digital services.

Bachelor of Science in Computational Modeling and Simulation


Program Overview

This interdisciplinary degree focuses on mathematical modeling, simulation techniques, and computational problem-solving across scientific and engineering domains. Students learn to translate real-world systems into digital models, enabling predictions, testing, and optimization through simulation. The curriculum includes applied mathematics, physics-based modeling, algorithm design, and software development.


Graduates are equipped to work in areas such as systems biology, environmental forecasting, engineering design, and urban planning. They gain experience using simulation tools like MATLAB and Simulink, as well as general-purpose programming languages, to create models that support innovation and decision-making in complex environments.

Bachelor of Applied Statistics with Data Science


Program Overview

The Bachelor of Applied Statistics with Data Science focuses on statistical theory and practical data applications. Students are trained in probability, regression analysis, hypothesis testing, and survey methodology, along with modern data science tools such as Python, R, and SQL. The goal is to produce professionals capable of extracting knowledge from data and solving real-world problems using statistical reasoning.


In addition to classroom instruction, students undertake fieldwork and projects involving demographic studies, risk modeling, and public health data. This degree opens doors to careers in government statistics offices, market research, healthcare analytics, and global development organizations where quantitative skills are in high demand.

Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering (AI Pathway)


Program Overview

This program merges core software engineering principles with artificial intelligence specialization. Students learn how to design, build, test, and maintain intelligent software systems with a focus on automation, scalability, and user experience. Core subjects include object-oriented programming, system architecture, AI frameworks, and cloud computing.


The AI pathway introduces advanced modules in natural language processing, intelligent agents, and human-AI interaction. With hands-on lab work, internships, and team-based projects, graduates are prepared to become AI software engineers, system architects, or product developers capable of delivering intelligent solutions across platforms.

Bachelor of Technology in AI and Robotics


Program Overview

This practical, tech-focused program trains students in designing autonomous systems, robotic hardware integration, and embedded AI technologies. Coursework includes robotics engineering, control systems, machine vision, and sensor integration alongside deep learning and intelligent decision-making techniques.


Students get hands-on experience building robotic prototypes and simulating real-world environments in labs. The program prepares graduates for roles in robotics development, industrial automation, drone technology, and smart manufacturing — fields where AI meets hardware to drive intelligent innovation.

Bachelor of Information Technology with Machine Intelligence


Program Overview

Blending IT infrastructure with intelligent computing, this program prepares students to build, manage, and secure data-driven systems that leverage machine intelligence. Core subjects cover networking, cybersecurity, systems administration, and software development, while advanced courses focus on intelligent data processing, anomaly detection, and AI-enhanced systems.


Graduates can pursue careers as IT specialists, system analysts, or AI-focused IT consultants who bring smart automation to enterprise environments. The program’s emphasis on applied intelligence in system operations makes it ideal for students interested in practical, tech-enabled problem-solving.
