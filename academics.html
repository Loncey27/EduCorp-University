Academics

Educorp University’s Department of Computational Intelligence and Data Technologies offers a comprehensive suite of degree programs across three levels of study — Undergraduate, Master’s, and Doctoral (PhD) — designed to equip learners with deep technical knowledge, research capabilities, and real-world problem-solving skills in data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

Not sure which specialty to pursue? Begin your journey through our Computing Exploratory Studies Pathway (CESP) — a flexible first-year experience that allows you to explore core concepts across all three fields while receiving expert guidance before selecting your major.
